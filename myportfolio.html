The code shown above was the code that me and my parnter wrote for our interactive fiction project. The story is quite long and absurd too. You begin with a normal quest to slay a dragon and have many questions such as your weapon of choice but then you may end up answering a question such as what should you name the band you started with your dragon. It ends with the main character(you) befriending the dragon instead of killing it which in my opinion is quite clever. We also put a few images in the code so depending on your answer, you might see a man with a bow and an arrow written in symbols or a dragon written in symbols.
